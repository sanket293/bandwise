This Privacy Policy explains how BandWise ("the app", "we", "us") handles your
information. BandWise is developed by **Sanket Vagadiya**. By using the app you
agree to the practices described here.

## Summary (plain English)

- BandWise does **not** require an account or sign-up.
- Your practice scores and logs are stored **only on your device**. We never
  see them, and they are never uploaded to us.
- The app shows ads through **Google AdMob**, which may collect certain device
  information (including your advertising ID) to serve and measure ads.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## Information the app stores

**Practice data (on your device only).**
When you enter scores, log practice sessions, or change settings, that
information is saved locally on your device so the app can show your history and
trends. This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us or to any
server we control. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information
is deleted.

**We do not collect personal information.**
BandWise does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location, and the
developer does not receive any of the data you enter into the app.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

BandWise displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**, a service of
Google LLC. To serve and measure ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and
process certain information from your device, which may include:

- Your device's **advertising identifier** (e.g. Android Advertising ID);
- Device and app information (device model, operating system, app version,
  general/coarse location inferred from IP, and ad-interaction data).

This information is used to deliver ads (personalized or non-personalized),
limit how often you see the same ad, and measure ad performance. The processing
of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time in your device settings
under **Settings → Google → Ads** (Android).

## Children's privacy

BandWise is intended for a general audience (people preparing for the IELTS
exam) and is **not directed at children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal
information through the app, please contact us and we will address it.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. The only third party
that receives data through the app is Google AdMob, as described above, for the
purpose of serving advertisements.

## Data security

Because your practice data is stored locally on your device, its security
depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We
recommend keeping your device software up to date and secured with a passcode.

## Your choices

- **Opt out of personalized ads:** adjust the ad settings on your device as
  described above.
- **Delete your data:** clear the app's data or uninstall the app to remove all
  locally stored information.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise
the "Effective date" at the top. Continued use of the app after changes take
effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
